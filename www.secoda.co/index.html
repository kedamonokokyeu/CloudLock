Why CloudLock?

Traditional manual audits are often time consuming and prone to human error. They are often expensive and have inconsistent standards. CloudLock provides a cost-effective solution with instant automated analysis. In addition, the webscraping detection can detect if private information is out on the web, notifying you through slack or email.
